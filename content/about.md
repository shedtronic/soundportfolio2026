# About

I am a sound designer and composer exploring the space between the atmospheric, the uncanny and the cinematic.  
My work blends contemporary sound design with hauntological and Radiophonic influences to create immersive soundworlds for film, games and experimental media.

## What I Do
- Atmospheric sound design  
- Composition and musical worldbuilding  
- Field recording & Foley  
- Experimental audio / glitch / generative systems  
- Interactive media and creative coding
