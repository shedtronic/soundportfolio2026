## Overview  
*Hattons Cross* is a fully developed animated short created during the COVID lockdown period.  
The project combines character-driven sound design, environmental atmospheres, musical cues, and integrated voice acting.

Working with writers, animators, and actors, I created the full sonic identity for the film â€” from dialogue edit to final mix.

## Creative Role  
- Sound Designer  
- Dialogue Editing / Cleanup  
- Composer  
- Foley & Atmospheric Design  
- Final Mix  

---

## Techniques Used  
- Spatial characterisation using convolution reverb  
- Layered Foley for footsteps, movement, and props  
- Emotional cue design using drones + thematic motifs  
- Noise reduction on remote-recorded VO  
- Scene-by-scene mix decisions for mood + pacing
